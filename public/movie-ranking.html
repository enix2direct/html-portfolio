The Best Movies According to David

My Top Three Action Movies of All Time

Terminator 2 - Judgement Day

The story, action, pacing, and visual effects weave a tale of a hopelessly outdated robot programmed the future savior of humanity against an unstoppable adversary hellbent on changing the future.

The Matrix

While the sequals never lived up to the hype created by the original, the story of a seemingly ordinary man, learning to use his inate abilities while facing down a trio of invincible advesaries make this movie a thrill ride worth watching on repeat!

Star Wars

You wouldn't think so today, but this movie started an entire genre of action space fantasy. This movie has everything, from laser pistols and the most iconic movie weapon ever made(the light saber) to androids running around on giant space ships. Star Wars still embodies the good vs. evil fight and does it in a way that had never been seen before.
